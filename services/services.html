Services

ANTAR Logistics operations cover a wide range of services, which has only been possible by fostering excellent business ties with numerous reputable concerns in commerce and industry.

 

Prime examples of this can be found in the partnerships established while trading in East Malaysia (consolidation) and the Oleo chemical industry as a whole (FOSFA / NIOP requirements). Similarly proficient and skilled customer relations with Oleo chemical shippers have also, and
continue to, benefit ANTAR.

The company’s Isotank operations and Flexitank, as a user and supplier, are well-known regionally and with cleaning depots found across Malaysia, freight negotiation services catered for, regular communications with liners, up-to-date sailing schedules, bulk shipment brokerage, tank farm operations, import and export clearance, and warehouse operations, among others, ANTAR has rightly positioned itself as one of the leading logistics providers.

Antar currently has a Global Combined Fleet of 20,000 units.

After Sales Service

Cleaning Process

Providing cleaning services for the isotanks are vital in our business as water condensation is unavoidable inside containers when it has been closed for a few days. With our friendly team of skilled container cleaners, we are ready to assist you by performing these cleaning services at our depot.

Valve Discharging

Upon arrival of import shipment at our designated port, we have many cases where valves are unable to be opened due to the long duration of shipment on board. We have ready field engineers to be deployed at site the moment this problem arises.

Shipment Clearance

Cases where shippers/consignees have difficulties clearing shipment from the customs due to tank detention. We provide free extension for storage shipment for our loyal customers. At the same time, we provide the right assistance to ensure that the shipment is cleared in due time.

Cargo Compatibility

We provide a study to understand better on the compatibility of the cargo on that particular container. The reason for this being is that a customer wouldn’t know best on the last shipment of the tank and the contents that were shipped with the container. Since we are accredited with FOSPA & NIOP’s standards, we are sure to provide you with a confirmation on the safety of your shipment placement.

Freight Booking

Booking of container shipment in the ship according to date, time and route. With our wide network of shippers, we are sure to place you accordingly. This service is also extended to air freight services of the isotank for urgent shipments.

Cross Border Trucking

We provide cross border trucking for when there is a need from a client on trans-shipment. We have currently completed our longest haul in journey from Port Klang, Malaysia right up to Bangkok,Thailand. Since we are located in Klang,Malaysia we are ready to provide this service to you anywhere around Malaysia, Singapore & Thailand.

Customs Brokerage

Through our customs brokerage service, we make arrangements & brokerage for the arrival of the isotank and reserve a spot in the liquid bulk terminal. Our current location for this services is in Malaysia and we have representatives globally to guide you through this process.

Isotank Loading, Storage & Leasing

We perform loading of isotanks on ships and at the same time store liquid that has been transferred from the ship to the liquid bulk terminal. We currently provide nitrogen blanketing for certain products and heating services whenever products have been hardened. Isotanks for lease are available and can also be used for permanent storage.

Cargo Insurance Coverage

We have currently tied ourselves with a global insurance company to provide all forms of insurance in the shipment of goods and all sections that are required by our client. Insurance is necessary during the shipment process of isotanks from one location to another.

Trans-Shipment

We currently provide this service for a host of clients for repackaging works and reshipment. For clients who choose to ship out existing shipment to other locations, bonded warehouses are recommended as there is no duty and tax to be paid. For clients who choose to repackage and perform shipment afterward, non-bonded warehouses are recommended. Transportation of the isotank to be sent within the Port and outward will be organized by us.

Flexi Bag Shipment

A rather new alternative to isotanks, flexi-bags are much cheaper and can fit perfectly into 20 footer containers. It has become a new alternative to many clients and we have performed the shipment of the flexibags to various countries. With the right packaging and planning, the flexi-bag stands strong next to the isotank.
